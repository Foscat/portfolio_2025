SMU - Full Stack Bootcamp

August 2018 - Feburary 2019

Overview

My experience at Southern Methodist University (SMU) was a transformative journey that served as my initial foray into the world of programming. Throughout my time at SMU, I honed fundamental skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js, Express.js, and React.js, laying the groundwork for my career as a developer. This dedicated page serves as a testament to the skills and knowledge acquired during my educational journey, showcasing three pivotal projects: "Gif Freak," my inaugural project where I delved into utilizing APIs; "Stock Memer," my first collaborative endeavor exploring group dynamics in development; and "Scion Matches," the culmination of my SMU experience, demonstrating my proficiency in teamwork and project management. Each project offers a glimpse into the lessons learned and the growth achieved during my time at SMU, highlighting the foundation upon which my career in software development has been built.

Gif Freak

"Gif Freak" is a dynamic and interactive website developed as part of my educational journey at SMU. Deployed on GitHub Pages, this project showcases my proficiency in HTML, JavaScript, jQuery, and Ajax. The website leverages the Giphy API to fetch and display gifs based on user input. Upon loading, the site presents users with pre-defined buttons, each triggering the display of the most relevant gif retrieved from the API. Additionally, users have the option to create custom buttons tailored to their preferences, initiating requests for and displaying a selection of up to 10 gifs associated with the specified search term. These user-generated buttons are dynamically added to the master list, enhancing the website's functionality and user experience. Notably, user-created buttons are only stored temporarily within the session, since I did not know a way to save information at that time. "Gif Freak" demonstrates my ability to integrate multiple technologies early in my education. While simple and quite rough to look at the code now this was the project that really lit my fire of inspiration as to what I could do as a developer and actually have fun when developing.

Stock Memer

"Stock Memer" represents a collaborative effort and marks my first venture into group project development during my time at SMU. This web application provides users with the ability to view real-time stock price data for companies listed on the Nasdaq stock exchange. Alongside the stock graph, users can access a summary of key information related to the selected company. Additionally, "Stock Memer" offers a unique feature allowing users to generate memes related to specific stocks and post them directly to the corresponding stock's page. Memes and their associations with stock IDs are stored in Google Firebase. Although the database functionality is no longer operational due to the expiration of the meme generator API key, preventing users from creating memes to save, the stock price/trend API system and graphs remain functional on the deployed version.

In this project, my primary responsibilities included creating the API and database interactions, while my teammates focused on designing the user interface and implementing graph generation based on the data retrieved from the APIs. "Stock Memer" exemplifies my ability to collaborate effectively within a team setting, showcasing my contributions to the project's backend development and database management.

Scion Matches

"Scion Matches" represents a significant milestone in my journey as a developer, serving as my final project at SMU. The website was conceived with the aim of revolutionizing the intricate process of matching Intended Parents (IPs) with Gestational Carriers (GCs) within the realm of surrogacy. Recognizing the substantial time and financial investments inherent in this journey, "Scion Matches" was designed to alleviate the burdens by facilitating connections based on compatibility and preferences. Users would create accounts and undergo a comprehensive survey, providing detailed insights into their backgrounds, desires, and expectations. Leveraging my expertise, I developed the matching algorithm that meticulously compared users' responses, generating match scores to identify the most compatible pairings. This algorithm formed the backbone of the platform, enabling seamless matchmaking and empowering users to request connections with utmost confidence.

Despite the deployment URL no longer being in service and the absence of archive screenshots, "Scion Matches" remains a pivotal project in my portfolio. It signifies a pivotal shift in my approach to software development, as I transitioned from building projects for mere enjoyment to creating solutions with tangible real-world value. "Scion Matches" embodies my commitment to leveraging technology to address pressing societal issues, underscoring my determination to make a meaningful impact through my work.
